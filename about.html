School History

The journey of RTPM-DSHS through the years

Foundation (1988)

The school was established in 1988 per Ordinance No. 81. It began as a single-level class temporarily located in Calindagan.

A Permanent Home (1991)

In 1991, the school moved to Villa Amada, Barangay Daro, on land donated by Ramon Teves-Pastor.
